iROV

Internet Remotely Operated Underwater Submersible Vehicle, or iROV for short. Designed to be a low cost, easy to use, and accessible underwater drone. The system is designed to be accessible to anyone who has internet access. You can use a keyboard, game controller or touchscreen.
